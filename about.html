About Sir C R REDDY

Sir C R Reddy College, established in 1945, is named after a distinguished educationalist and the founding Vice-Chancellor of Andhra University. It offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs under the Sir C R Reddy Educational Institutions society.

Affiliation and Recognition
Affiliated with Adikavi Nannaya University since 2015. Recognized by UGC under Act 2(f) and 12(B) since 1969. Granted autonomy from 1987-88 to 2031-32.

Accreditation and Certifications
NAAC accredited at 'A' level in 2005, 2011, 2017, and 2022. Designated as College with Potential for Excellence by UGC in 2010. ISO 9001:2015 certified.

Infrastructure
Includes Degree Blocks, PG Blocks (A, B, C), Central Library, Auditorium, Gymnasium, Bank, Canteen, Dining Hall, Distance Education Block.

Hostels and Security
Separate hostels for boys and girls with on-campus security.

Conclusion
The college continues its legacy of academic excellence, offering students a well-rounded education in a modern, secure campus.
