WELCOME TO OUR COLLEGE

Sir C. Ramalinga Reddy College, one of the premier Educational Institutions in Andhra Pradesh, was established on 4th July 1945 owing to the relentless efforts made by educationists, senior citizens and philanthropic benefactors of this part of the West Godavari District. We believe in the transformational power of a college education to impart knowledge and enlightenment in a global society. We believe higher education is a privilege, and that an educated person has a responsibility not only to seek personal and professional success but also to contribute to a greater good.
